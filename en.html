Preparation Work

A good result starts with preparation work. Washing, scraping, protective covering and other preparations directly affect how the painting looks and how long it lasts. The project often begins with a mould wash, where the house is thoroughly washed with mould removal detergent and brushes. This removes black mould spots, dust and other dirt. Preparation work also includes removing flaking paint, priming exposed wood areas and protecting the surroundings from paint splashes.

Small Surface Repairs

Not every property only needs paint. Sometimes general tidying up, small repairs and other maintenance-related work are needed as well. For example, fascia boards and window trims often become worn and may need replacing. Small maintenance jobs done at the right time help keep the entire building looking better and can prevent larger repair costs in the future.

Frequently Asked Questions About Exterior Painting

How often should a house be painted?

Usually every 10 to 15 years, but the maintenance interval depends on the surface, paint, weather and how well previous treatments were done.

Is washing needed before painting?

Yes. The surface must be cleaned before painting so that the new coating adheres properly and the result lasts.

Can an old surface be painted directly?

Sometimes, but often the paint surface is partly in poor condition, in which case preparation work is needed. That is why the property should be assessed before the work begins.

How long does painting usually take?

The duration depends on the size of the property, weather and the amount of preparation work, but a typical detached house is often completed in about 3 to 5 days.

Can painting be done in rainy or damp weather?

Usually not. Excess moisture weakens the result, so the right weather window is an important part of successful painting.

How much does painting cost?

The price depends on the size, condition and amount of preparation work. Painting a typical detached house usually costs around €3,000–€5,000.

Can painting increase the value of a house?

A neat exterior improves the overall appearance of the building and can increase interest in a sales situation.
